# kcolbchain research

Published research notes, analysis, and briefs from the kcolbchain research collective (est. 2015).

## Focus Areas

- **Protocol design & cryptoeconomics** — consensus, mechanism design, governance
- **Security & formal methods** — smart contract security, audits, formal verification
- **Zero-knowledge systems** — proving systems, ZKVMs, circuit engineering
- **DeFi & financial primitives** — AMMs, lending, yield, tokenisation, MEV
- **AI x Blockchain** — verifiable inference, on-chain agents, decentralised compute
- **Scalability & data availability** — rollups, DA layers, parallel execution
- **Payments & stablecoins** — stablecoin design, regulation, payment infrastructure
- **RWA tokenisation** — treasuries, private credit, institutional adoption
