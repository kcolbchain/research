## ⚠️ Disclaimer & context (read first)

- **Research, not product.** These are *research papers* — analysis, protocol design, and proposals from an open-source research collective. They describe directions and prototypes, not shipped, audited, or production systems. Where a paper discusses a design-stage idea, it is labelled as such.
- **Forward-looking material is a model, not a forecast.** Any economic figures, throughput numbers, adoption scenarios, or projections in these papers are clearly-labelled **models with stated assumptions** — not forecasts, promises, or commitments. Read the assumptions before reading the numbers.
- **Regulatory gating.** Anything touching regulated rails (CBDC / e₹, stablecoins, licensed assets) is design-stage and **subject to the approvals and licences we will seek from the relevant regulators**. Where a paper references e₹, note that e₹ is the Reserve Bank of India's CBDC and is controlled by the RBI; nothing here is live or licensed. Named counterparties are *stated, not finalised* unless a paper says otherwise.
- **Claims are grounded.** External claims should be traceable to a cited source; on-chain / repo claims should be checkable against the named org or repository. If you find a claim that is not, that is a bug — please open an issue.
- **Attribution & licence.** Ideas and initiatives here are by Pattermesh (P. Sundaram), partner — KCOLBCHAIN, with Abhishek Krishna (@abhicris). All content is published under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). Cite as: *"kcolbchain Research, [title], June 2026."*
